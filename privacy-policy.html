AY Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Please read this Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, share, protect, and process your information when you access our website or engage with our services. We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of individuals visiting or interacting with our website at https://www.aytradingco.com/.

1. Information We Collect From You

We collect information that you voluntarily provide as well as data gathered automatically:

a) Information You Provide

When you fill out a form, request details, sign up for updates, or contact us, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City or location
  • Company or business details (if shared)
  • Any message or inquiry content

This information helps us respond to your request and deliver relevant services.

b) Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the AY website, we may use cookies and tracking tools to collect:

  • Browser type
  • Pages you view
  • Time spent on the site
  • Click patterns
  • Device information and IP address

This helps us improve website performance and personalize your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Your Information?

We use your information for clear and legitimate purposes, including:

  • To handle your requests and provide the services you require.
  • To share information, updates, or promotional content, you have opted into.
  • To improve site performance and ensure visitors have a better browsing experience.
  • To run analytics, measure performance, and understand user preferences.
  • To customize communication and marketing messages.
  • To maintain security, prevent unauthorized use, and comply with legal requirements.

We do not use your information for purposes unrelated to our services or operations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

a) Service Partners

Trusted third-party vendors assisting with hosting, marketing, analytics, communication, or customer support may receive certain information needed to perform their services.

b) Advertising & Analytics

We may share non-sensitive data with authorized marketing or analytics partners to improve the relevance of our ads and content.

c) Legal or Regulatory Requirements

Your information may be shared if required by law, government authorities, or for protection against fraud or security threats.

Please note: AY does not sell your personal data to any unauthorized party.

4. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain your information only for as long as required for the purpose it was collected or as required by law.

You may request:

  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Update of your details
  • Removal or deletion of your information
  • Withdrawal of any previously given consent

Note: Some information may need to be retained for compliance or verification.

5. Protection & Security Measures

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. However, data transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed as entirely secure.

We encourage you to:

  • Use secure devices
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information through unsecured channels
  • Review your details before submitting forms

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your information; however, no online transmission is 100% secure.

6. Use of Cookies

Cookies help us deliver a smoother and more customized browsing experience.

Through cookies, we may:

  • Improve loading speed
  • Recognize returning visitors
  • Analyze user behaviour
  • Personalize website features and ads

You can adjust or turn off cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may limit certain website features.

7. Consent

By submitting your information or using the AY website, you:

  • Agree that the information you provide is accurate.
  • Allow us to use your information as described in this policy.
  • Confirm that you have the authority to share information that does not belong to you.

You may withdraw consent at any time.

8. Contact Details

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to update or delete your information, you can contact us at:

  • Email: info@aytradingco.com
  • Phone: (+91) 1234 5678 90
  • Working Hours: Monday–Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM
